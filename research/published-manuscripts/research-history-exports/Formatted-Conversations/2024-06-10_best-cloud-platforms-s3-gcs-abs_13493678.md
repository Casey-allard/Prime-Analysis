For storing 18,000 large picture files cost-effectively and with ease of transfer, you might want to consider cloud storage platforms like Amazon S3 (Simple Storage Service), Google Cloud Storage, or Microsoft Azure Blob Storage. Here's a brief overview of each:

It provides SDKs and tools like AzCopy for efficient data transfer to and from the storage account.

All three options have their own advantages and pricing structures, so I recommend comparing their features, pricing, and documentation to determine which one best fits your specific requirements and budget. Additionally, consider factors like data redundancy, availability, and access controls when making your decision.
