### Assistant (2025-11-14 20:02:47)

Yes. In *Life of Pi*, the adult Pi Patel lives in **Toronto**.

A few key points from the novel:

- After the shipwreck and rescue, Pi **emigrates to Canada** with his family (or what remains of it).
- He eventually settles in **Toronto**, where he grows into adulthood.
- The fictional “author” character in the book meets Pi while Pi is living in Toronto.
- Pi also **studies at the University of Toronto**.

So while Pi is originally from **Pondicherry, India**, his adult life — including the frame narrative — is set in **Toronto, Ontario**.
