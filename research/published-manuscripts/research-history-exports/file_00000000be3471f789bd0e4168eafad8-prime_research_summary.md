# Prime Admissibility as a Recursive Dynamical System

## Overview
This document summarizes the key findings from exploratory research into prime-tuple admissibility under modular transport. The goal is to identify compressed predictive structure within prime-generated combinatorial systems.

---

## Definitions

Let A be a fixed admissible prime tuplet.

Define the wheel:
W_r = 2·3·5·7·∏ q_m

Transport length:
L_r = W_r / 6

Phase:
j ∈ Z / L_r Z

Mapping:
n(j) = 5 + 6j (mod W_r)

Admissibility:
w_r(j) = 1 iff gcd(n(j)+a, W_r) = 1 ∀ a ∈ A
