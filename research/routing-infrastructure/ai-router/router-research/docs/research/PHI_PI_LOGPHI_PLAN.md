## Purpose
Turn the recent controller results into a clean geometric program instead of another round of unstructured tuning.

## Principle
Do not force one constant to do every job.
The route now has enough evidence to separate the geometry into layers.

## Layer 1: Continuous Angular Geometry Uses `pi`
Use `pi` where the object is actually angular.

## Layer 2: Continuous Radial Growth Uses Hyperbolic Expansion
Use hyperbolic / exponential growth for the time-expanded radial field.

## Layer 3: Discrete Controller Uses `phi` and `log(phi)`
Use `phi` where the architecture decides how to branch, widen, merge, and stabilize.
Use `log(phi)` where the controller must accumulate or release pressure additively.
